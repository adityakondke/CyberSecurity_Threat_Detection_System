What You Can Detect (through Firewall Logs)

Multiple Failed Connections

Repeated DENY entries or many connection attempts → brute-force / port-scan.

Unusual IP Activity

Foreign or rarely-seen IPs initiating connections → suspicious source.

High Traffic Volume

Large number of requests from single IP → possible DDoS or flood.

Access to Restricted Ports

Attempts to reach admin/RDP ports (3389,22, etc.) → privilege probing.

Continuous Packet Drops

Frequent DROP/REJECT/ICMP issues → network blocking or scanning.
